History

We started with a dream to help poor fur-babies get a second chance at

Happily Ever After!

Our puppies are found, surrendered, and saved by volunteers just like YOU! We are a non-profit where all donations are used to help the animals we save.

Location

We are located throughout the greater Chicago area! Our foster families provide loving homes to our adoptable pets all across the city. Please use the contact us link to to find a friend near you.
